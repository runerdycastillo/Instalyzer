how to download your instagram data

This page is the detailed walkthrough for getting your Instagram export. The landing page stays focused on the product story; this guide handles the step-by-step help.

open instagram back to Instalyzer

written walkthrough

These steps are for Instagram on desktop. Once your file is ready, come back to Instalyzer and upload it to start analyzing your dataset.

  1. 01 open your profile menu

    Go to your Instagram profile on desktop and click the three-line menu in the lower-left sidebar.

  2. 02 open settings

    Choose settings, then open the accounts center area if Instagram sends you there.

  3. 03 find your information

    Click your information and permissions, then select download your information.

  4. 04 choose the account and format

    Pick the Instagram account you want to export, choose the data range you need, and select JSON format before creating the file.

  5. 05 download the archive

    Once Instagram finishes preparing the export, download the zip directly from the page.

  6. 06 come back and upload it here

    Return to Instalyzer, click get started, and upload the downloaded export to build your dataset.

quick note

If Instagram changes the wording, look for paths related to your information, permissions, or downloading your data.

recommended export settings

  • Format: JSON
  • Date range: all time for relationship tools
  • Best use: upload once, reuse the dataset across tools
