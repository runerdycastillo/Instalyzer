download your instagram data

follow the steps below, then upload your export into Instalyzer.

quick guide

  1. 01

    open your instagram profile

    Log into Instagram on desktop and open your profile.

  2. 02

    go to settings

    Open the menu, then go to Settings and Accounts Center.

  3. 03

    open your information and permissions

    Find the section where Instagram lets you manage and download your data.

  4. 04

    select download your information

    Choose the option to request your Instagram export.

  5. 05

    choose JSON and All time

    Set the format to JSON, choose All time, and keep media quality on Medium.

  6. 06

    download the ZIP and upload it

    When your file is ready, download the ZIP and come back to Instalyzer to upload it.

before you start

recommended settings

  • Date range: All time
  • Format: JSON
  • Media quality: Medium

common issues

  • No file? Check email and spam.
  • Wrong format? Use JSON.
  • Upload issue? Use the export ZIP file.
